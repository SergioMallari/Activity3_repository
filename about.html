History:

H&M was founded in 1947 by Erling Persson in Västerås, Sweden. The company's name is derived from the initials of the founder's father, Hennes (Swedish for "hers") and the word "Mauritz," representing the acquisition of a hunting and fishing equipment store.



Affordable Fashion, High Quality:

H&M is synonymous with accessible fashion without compromising on quality. Our design teams work tirelessly to bring the latest trends to our stores while maintaining high standards of craftsmanship. We believe that looking good shouldn't break the bank, and our commitment to affordability has made us a favorite among fashion-conscious shoppers worldwide.
